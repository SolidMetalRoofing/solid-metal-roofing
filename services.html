Roofing Services

Metal and shingle roofing solutions for every property type — residential, commercial, and agricultural.

Residential Metal Roofing

Your home is your biggest investment. A metal roof protects it for decades with virtually zero maintenance — while adding curb appeal that shingle roofs can't match.

We install standing seam panels, metal shingles, and metal tile profiles in a wide range of colours to complement your home's architecture. Every residential project includes a detailed site assessment, written quote, and warranty documentation before we start.

Popular for: New construction, re-roofs over existing shingles, hail damage replacement, energy-efficient upgrades.

Get a Residential Quote

Commercial Roofing

Metal roofing systems for shops, offices, warehouses, and retail buildings. Engineered for large-span coverage, low maintenance, and long-term performance.

We work with property managers, business owners, and general contractors to deliver roofing systems that protect your assets and reduce operating costs. Metal's thermal efficiency and durability make it the smart choice for any commercial building in Alberta.

Popular for: Shops, warehouses, retail, strip malls, multi-unit buildings.

Get a Commercial Quote

Agricultural Roofing

Farmers know — a good roof on your barn or shop saves you money, time, and headaches for decades. Metal is the standard for agricultural buildings because nothing else comes close for durability and weather resistance.

We roof barns, quonsets, livestock shelters, equipment shops, grain bins, and riding arenas. Our panels are rated for heavy snow loads, high winds, and the UV exposure that comes with wide-open Alberta farmland.

Popular for: Barns, quonsets, shops, shelters, riding arenas, grain storage.

Get an Agricultural Quote

Asphalt Shingle Roofing

While we specialize in metal, we know shingles are the right choice for some projects and budgets. We install high-quality architectural shingles from trusted manufacturers — with the same attention to detail we bring to every metal job.

If you're weighing metal vs. shingles, we'll walk you through the costs and benefits of each so you can make the best decision for your property. No pressure, just honest information.

Repairs & Maintenance

Existing roof need attention? We handle repairs, flashing replacement, fastener re-torques, panel replacements, and emergency leak repairs across Alberta.

Leak Repairs

Fast diagnosis and permanent fixes for metal and shingle roof leaks. No Band-Aid solutions.

Storm Damage

Hail, wind, and storm damage assessment and repair. We work with your insurance company.

Re-Roofing

Full tear-off and replacement when repair isn't enough. Upgrade from shingles to metal in one project.

Inspections

Pre-purchase, annual, and post-storm roof inspections with a detailed written report.
