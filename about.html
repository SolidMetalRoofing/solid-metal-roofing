About Solid Metal Roofing

Alberta-owned. Built on the belief that every property deserves a roof that can take whatever this province throws at it.

Our Story

Solid Metal Roofing started from a simple idea: Alberta homeowners and farmers deserve roofing that doesn't need replacing every 15 years. After years working in the trades and seeing roofs fail after every hailstorm season, we decided to build a company focused exclusively on metal — the material that outlasts everything else.

Today, we serve residential, commercial, and agricultural clients across the province. Every project gets the same level of care: proper planning, quality materials, clean installation, and a warranty you can actually count on.

We're not the biggest roofing company in Alberta. We're the one that shows up, does it right, and stands behind the work.
