Why Metal Roofing?

Homeowners and farmers across Alberta are switching to metal for one simple reason — it pays for itself.

50+ Year Lifespan

Metal roofing outlasts shingles 2–3 times over. Install once and never worry about a re-roof during your lifetime.

Energy Savings

Reflective metal panels reduce cooling costs by up to 25%. Your roof works for you year-round.

Hail & Wind Rated

Class 4 impact-rated panels withstand golf-ball-sized hail and wind gusts over 180 km/h. Built for Alberta.

Increases Home Value

A new metal roof recovers up to 85% of its cost at resale. Buyers want durability — and they'll pay for it.

Metal Roofing vs. Shingles

See why property owners across Alberta are choosing metal over traditional asphalt shingles.

Feature Metal Roofing ✦ Asphalt Shingles
Lifespan 40–70 years 15–25 years
Hail Resistance Class 4 (highest) Class 1–2
Wind Rating Up to 200 km/h Up to 110 km/h
Maintenance Near zero Regular inspections & patches
Energy Efficiency Reflects heat, saves 25% Absorbs heat
Fire Rating Class A (non-combustible) Class A–C (varies)
Environmental 100% recyclable Landfill waste
